Find the gross price of ordering a movie online if the net price is €5 + VAT at 21%.
We want
Gross price = 121% (Net + VAT)
Into calculator
5 × 1.21
Gross price = €6.05
U.11 Pattern: Net → Gross
Net % = 100%
Gross % = 121%

Multiply by gross %
× 1.21
U.9 Does it make sense?
€5 + 21% ≈ €6 ✓

Car costs €9,000 including VAT at 25%.
(a) Net price? (b) VAT paid?
(a) We want net price = 100%
9000 ÷ 1.25
Net price = €7,200
(b) VAT paid
9000 − 7200
VAT = €1,800
U.11 Pattern: Gross → Net
Gross % = 125%
Net % = 100%

Divide by gross %
÷ 1.25
U.9 Does it make sense?

12
Phone covers: net €8 + €12 = €20. Gross = €24.
Find VAT rate.
We want
VAT rate = ?%
Into calculator
24 ÷ 20 = 1.2 = 120%
VAT rate = 20%
U.11 Pattern
Gross ÷ Net = Gross %
24 ÷ 20 = 1.2 = 120%

Gross % − 100% = VAT %
120% − 100% = 20%
U.9 Does it make sense?
€20 × 1.2 = €24 ✓
