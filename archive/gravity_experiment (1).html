Mandatory Experiment

Acceleration Due to Gravity

Measuring g by Free Fall

40 marks

Experiment Overview

In an experiment to measure the acceleration due to gravity g, the time t for a small, dense sphere to fall from rest through a height s was measured.


The procedure was repeated for a number of different heights.

Recorded Data

s (cm) 25 35 45 55 65 75 85
t (ms) 226 267 303 335 365 391 416

Questions

  • (i) Draw a labelled diagram of the arrangement of the apparatus used in this experiment. (12)
  • (ii) Explain why a small, dense sphere was used in this experiment.
  • (iii) Draw a suitable graph, on graph paper, to show the relationship between s and t.

Questions (continued)

  • (iv) Use the graph to calculate a value for g. (21)
  • (v) State a possible source of error in measuring:
    (a) the height, s
    (b) the time, t
    (7)
