Equation of a Line

← → Space
y/x = m
Slope (m): 1

For any point (x, y) on a line through the origin:

slope = rise/run = y/x = m

Rearranging: y = mx

y − 2 = 1(x − 3)
Point: (3, 2)    |    Slope: 1
🔴 Red = Translator (drag to move line) 🔵 Blue = Rotator (drag to change slope)

For any point (x, y) on a line through (x₁, y₁):

(y − y₁)/(x − x₁) = m

Rearranging gives Point-Slope Form:

y − y₁ = m(x − x₁)
