Question 1: Volume of Cylinders

Find the volume of each cylinder. V = πr²h

(i) r = 11 m, h = 10 m (π = 3.14)

V = πr²h
V = 3.14 × (11)² × 10
V = 3.14 × 121 × 10
V = 3799.4 m³

(ii) r = 8 mm, h = 14 mm (π = 22/7)

V = πr²h
V = 227 × (8)² × 14
V = 227 × 64 × 14
V = 22 × 64 × 147 = 197127
V = 2816 mm³

(iii) r = 5 cm, h = 10 cm (π = 3.14)

V = πr²h
V = 3.14 × (5)² × 10
V = 3.14 × 25 × 10
V = 785 cm³

(iv) r = 2.5 cm, h = 10 cm (in terms of π)

V = πr²h
V = π × (2.5)² × 10
V = π × 6.25 × 10
V = 62.5π cm³

Question 11: Ornament from Block

(a) Length of side of one cube

Total height = 2 spheres + 2 cubes = 24 cm
Let side of cube = s = diameter of sphere
2s + 2s = 24
4s = 24
s = 6 cm

(b) Volume of ornament (to 2 dp)

r = 6 ÷ 2 = 3 cm

V = 2 × s³ + 2 × 43πr³
V = 2 × (6)³ + 2 × 43 × π × (3)³
V = 2 × 216 + 83 × π × 27
V = 432 + 72π
V = 432 + 226.19
V = 658.19 cm³

(c) Percentage carved away (to 2 dp)

Original block volume = 6 × 6 × 24 = 864 cm³
Material removed = 864 − 658.19 = 205.81 cm³
Percentage = 205.81864 × 100
23.82%

Question 16: Cylinder Ratios

CSAsmall = 2πrh

Ratio = 2πrh8πrh = 14
