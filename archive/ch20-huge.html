Introduction
Circle with centre (h, k) and radius r:
(x − h)2 + (y − k)2 = r2
Centre at Origin:   x2 + y2 = r2
💡 Point on x-axis like (5, 0) or (−5, 0) → r = |x| immediately!
💡 Point on y-axis like (0, 3) or (0, −3) → r = |y| immediately!
💡 3-4-5 Triangle:   3² + 4² = 9 + 16 = 25 = 5²   — Know this!

Circle has A(−5, 0), B(5, 0), P(−4, k). Find equation, k, prove ∠APB = 90°.
Solution
💡 A(−5, 0) is on the x-axis → r = |−5| = 5 immediately!
(a)
x2 + y2 = 52 = 25
Point on axis!
(b)
52 = 42 + k2 → 25 = 16 + k2
3-4-5 again!
k2 = 9 → k = 3
k > 0
(c)
Slope AP = 3/1 = 3    Slope BP = 3/−9 = −⅓
Product = −1
3 × (−⅓) = −1   ∴ ⊥ at P ✓
