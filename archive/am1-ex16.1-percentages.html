A bag contains red balls and blue balls. If 35% of the balls are blue, what percentage of the balls are red?
Simpler version
There are only two colours: red and blue.
Blue = 35 out of every 100.
What about red?
35% blue
? red
100% total → blue + red = 100

Miriam has a certain amount of money in her bank account at the beginning of the week.

During the week she carries out the following transactions:
● She buys goods online for four amounts: €30, €45, €10 and €400.
● She withdraws €400 from an ATM.
● She lodges 20% of the total amount withdrawn from her account.
At the end of the week her balance is €507·88.
(a) Find:
(i) The total value of the online purchases
(ii) The total value of the withdrawals made during the week
(iii) The amount lodged to the account
(b) Work the problem backwards to find out how much money was in Miriam's account at the beginning of the week.
💳 ONLINE
BANKING
Simpler version
Miriam's week:

Money OUT (spent/withdrawn):
• Online shopping: €30 + €45 + €10 + €400
• ATM cash: €400

Money IN (lodged back):
• She puts back 20% of everything that left her account

At the end: €507.88 left

Questions:
(a) Add up the spending, add up all withdrawals, find 20% of that
(b) Work backwards: what did she start with?
