Equation of a Line

← → Space
y/x = m
Slope (m): 1

For any point (x, y) on a line through the origin:

slope = rise/run = y/x = m

Rearranging: y = mx

y − 1 = 1(x − 1)
Point 1: (1, 1)     Point 2: (5, 5)    |    Slope: 1

For a line through two points (x₁, y₁) and (x₂, y₂):

slope m = (y₂ − y₁) / (x₂ − x₁)

Using point-slope form with either point:

y − y₁ = m(x − x₁)
Area = ½|x₁y₂ − x₂y₁|
Area = 0 square units
